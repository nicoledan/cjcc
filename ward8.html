Ward 8

Walker has seen a tremendous amount of crime since living in Ward 8 over the last few decades. She says that around five or six people were dying everyday in her ward during the 1980s and although crime she says has significantly improved, it still isn’t great. In 2014 Walker’s son was shot just a block away from where she lived. Her son just happened to be at the wrong place at the wrong time and was struck in the back by a bullet from a random shooting. Luckily he survived, but Antoinette still fears for her safety in her own community.
